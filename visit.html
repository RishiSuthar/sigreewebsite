Getting Here

Easy to Reach,
Worth the Trip

By Car

We're on Rosebank Road, just off the Northwestern Motorway (SH16). Take the Rosebank Road exit and head south. Free street parking is available along Rosebank Road and in surrounding side streets.

By Bus

Several bus routes service Rosebank Road. The nearest stop is a short walk from our front door. Check Auckland Transport for current timetables.

Accessibility

Our restaurant is accessible at street level. If you have specific access requirements, give us a call and we'll make sure everything is ready for your arrival.
