The Story

A Passion for Authentic
Indian Cuisine

The sigree is a traditional Indian charcoal brazier. It's been at the centre of Indian cooking for centuries. Long before gas jets and induction hobs, skilled cooks knew that live fire produced something nothing else could: deep, smoky caramelisation, juicy interiors, and the unmistakable smell of charcoal doing its thing.

Sigree Indian Cuisine was founded because Auckland deserved a restaurant where those techniques were taken seriously. Not as a novelty, but as the actual way the food gets cooked. Our chefs have roots across India, from the tandoor kitchens of Punjab to the coastal seafood traditions of Goa, and that shows in every section of the menu.

We're in the heart of Avondale, and we've built a place where families, couples, and groups can sit down to food that's rooted in tradition but doesn't feel stuck in the past.

How We Cook

The Craft Behind Every Dish

Live Charcoal Fire

Everything we grill passes over real charcoal. The heat is patient and precise, producing a crust and smokiness that only open fire can get you. No shortcuts, no substitutes.

Layered Marinades

Our marinades are built in stages: tenderise first, then flavour, then seal. Proteins rest overnight so the taste goes all the way through, not just on the surface.

Regional Spice Craft

India's culinary landscape is vast. Our menu draws from multiple regions: the cream-rich gravies of the North, the aromatic coconut curries of the South, and the street-spiced chaat of the West.

Fresh Daily Prep

Sauces, breads, and spice blends are made fresh each day. Nothing is pre-made from a jar. Our kitchen starts early and works methodically so that every service is served at its peak.

Carefully Plated

We believe presentation honours the food. Each plate is assembled with attention to colour, balance, and portion. The first bite is always with the eyes.

Warm, Attentive Service

Our team is trained to be present without being intrusive. We learn preferences, remember regulars, and make every guest feel genuinely welcome from the moment they walk in.
