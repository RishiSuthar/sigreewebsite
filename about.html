Who We Are

Rooted in Indian Tradition,
Crafted for Today

Sigree Barbeque began with a simple ambition: to honour authentic Indian grilling traditions while presenting them with the care and elegance expected of contemporary fine dining.

Our chefs work with charcoal fire, layered marinades, and regionally inspired spice profiles to bring depth and balance to every plate. Each detail, from plating to pacing, is designed for memorable evenings.
