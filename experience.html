The Experience

More Than a Meal,
It's an Occasion

At Sigree, the food is only part of it. The warmth of a real fire, unhurried service, and a room that actually feels good to be in. Whether it's lunch or a late sit-down, this is what eating out should be.

Your Visit

From the Moment
You Walk In

You'll be greeted by name if you've booked, and welcomed warmly either way. Our front-of-house team seats you, walks you through the menu, and gives honest recommendations. There's no rush. The kitchen works at its own pace, and the food arrives when it's ready.

Starters hit the table with the kind of aroma that makes the next table look over. Mains from the sigree carry a smokiness that no oven can replicate. And if you've got room, dessert is worth staying for. The whole thing just flows naturally. No performance, no fuss, just good food and a good time.

Whether you're in for a quick weekday lunch, a lazy Saturday with friends, or a proper sit-down celebration, the room works for all of it. That's the whole point.

How to Dine With Us

Every Table Has
Its Own Story

01

Date Night

Low lighting, warm tones, and food that gives you something to talk about. Book a corner table, order a couple of starters to share, and let it go from there. This is what Sigree does best.

02

Family Gatherings

Big tables, generous portions, and a menu that covers everyone, from the adventurous eaters to the kids who just want naan and butter chicken. Families are a big part of our regulars, and we take that seriously.

03

Private Dining

For groups of 8 to 40, we can set up a private or semi-private area with a tailored menu. Birthdays, engagements, anniversaries. Tell us what you need and we'll sort it.

04

Corporate & Business

Team dinners, client entertaining, end-of-year functions. We offer set menus, dedicated service, and a room that makes a good impression without trying too hard.

05

Chef's Table

A multi-course set menu based on whatever the kitchen is most excited about that week. Available for small groups. Ask about drink matching when you book.

06

Takeaway

Can't make it in? Our full menu is available for collection. Same kitchen, same care, packed for the journey home. Call ahead or order when you arrive.
