Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Refined for Today

The sigree—a traditional Indian charcoal grill—has been the heart of Indian culinary craft for generations. Its slow, smoky heat creates flavours that cannot be replicated by any modern method.

At Sigree Indian Cuisine, we honour this tradition while embracing the precision and presentation of contemporary fine dining. Every dish tells a story of heritage, prepared with ingredients sourced at their peak and served in an atmosphere designed for memorable occasions.

01

Flame & Time

Our sigree grill masters understand that exceptional flavour comes from patience. Each cut is marinated, rested, and grilled to precise specifications.

02

Regional Craft

Beyond the grill, our menu celebrates India's diverse culinary regions—from the rich gravies of the north to the aromatic curries of the coast.

03

Gracious Service

True hospitality is invisible yet ever-present. Our team is trained to anticipate needs and create an atmosphere of warmth and refinement.
